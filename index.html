Tom Hanks

Biography

Thomas Jeffrey Hanks (born July 9, 1956) is an American actor and filmmaker widely recognized as one of the most popular and influential film stars in the world. Known for both comedic and dramatic performances, he has become an American cultural icon. Hanks is the fourth-highest-grossing American film actor and the recipient of numerous honors, including:

  1. 2 Academy Awards
  2. 7 Emmy Awards
  3. 4 Golden Globe Awards
  4. AFI Life Achievement Award (2002)
  5. Kennedy Center Honor (2014)
  6. Presidential Medal of Freedom (2016)
  7. Cecil B. DeMille Award (2020)
  8. Nominated for five BAFTA Awards and a Tony Award.
